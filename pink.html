Cipe Pineles Designer Promo

This project was to create a type-dominant promo showcasing the work or lifetime of a specific designer and invite the viewer to a hypothetical showcase of their work.

I chose Cipe Pineles who was an underrated publication designer that revolutionized how mass media was marketed to teenage girls and also paved a way for women in graphic design.

The Secret History Title Sequence

This project was my first experience in Adobe Premier, but it was refreshing to create a title sequence for one of my favorite books as if it were turned into a movie.

Loader GIFS

In this project the assignment was to create three unique GIFs that could be used as loaders, something better than just a rotating circle or three dots that change color. We were also tasked with animating the GIFs in Photoshop frame by frame, so the results are a little choppier than if they were animated in After Effects. For my loaders, I used a bow and arrow morph, orange juice being poured, and paper being crumpled up.

The Modern Monster

This project was for an English class based around comic books. I had to create a prototype for an art comic. My comic idea, called The Modern Monster, is centered around four young women in a world where everyone is a monster instead of a human. The comic is meant as a slice of life for the modern young woman that would give advice on issues ranging from recognizing emotional abuse in relationships to making friends in college.

The four main characters pictured on this character sheet are Regan O'Connor, a demon, Samera Williams, a ghost, Valentine "V" Wu, a succubus, and Lily Simons, a swamp monster. I also designed the title for the comic, and hope to be able to produce a full issue as a passion project.

Character Design

This assignment was to create a character and list some facts about them. Because it was so broad, I decided to combine two of my ideas, a sword fighter and a scuba diver. My references included swimmer's legs and fencers arms, so it was interesting to combine the two in one body. I also had fun combining the scuba mask with the fencer's helmet.

Riot Grrrl Ethnography

This final project was to create a visual ethnography of subculture, whether ethnically or culturally. The subculture I chose was 90's feminst punk, or Riot Grrrl. In three pages I visually showed different bands, feminist ideas, and zines popular in the movement.
