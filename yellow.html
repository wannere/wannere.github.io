Carryall Packaging

This project was the first week of a personal challenge to work on different design projects in between my junior and senior years. I created a list of theme ideas and project types, and for this week I chose the theme "picnic" with the project type "packaging". Ergo, this is packaging for picnic type dining. The meal comes with Greek pasta salad, hummus with roasted veggies, rosemary pita wedges, and unsweetened iced tea. Yum!

Karma Wine

In this final project, our task was to focus on texture and explore type, shape, photos, and analogue methods. I first explored typography and photos in two separate wine redesigns, but the class unanimously agreed that I should move forward with photos. Using the texture on the back of a dumpster, I took a brand of wine called Karma and redesigned it into boxed wine with a sophisticated touch. This project also included a 3 minute pitch presentation as well as market research to influence the design.

Broody Gaming

Ice Cold Magazine

This project was to create a series of magazine covers. I created a music magazine called Ice Cold. The first issue has been expanded with an inside article.

The Academic

In January 2016 I was selected as a designer to work with several other students on the very first issue of an online zine called The Academic. It is about study techniques and succeeding throughout high school and college.

As a designer, I was tasked with creating a cover for the magazine as well as put together layouts for articles. Because every designer made a possible cover, we voted on which one to use.
