Snowledge App Enhancements

arrow_back
Prev

Snowledge is an app focused on snow sport metric tracking and mountain resort engagement. They wanted to add additional social capabilities for the app to turn it into a one stop shop for the slopes. My team at Roboboogie worked to provide a much-needed style guide update, UX, UI, and content recommendations to accommodate the new post types, in addition to being available for miscellaneous design fixes and best practice guidance.

Role

I created UX designs for this project and handed them off to another designer, then provided feedback, suggestions, and final approval as she finished UI designs.

Duration

10 weeks

Tools

Adobe XD

User Pain Points

  • Photos and runs are the only two types of content to post
  • Tracking run screen is too confusing
  • Notifications are hard to find

Goals

  • Provide new UI to integrate new post types
  • Fix areas with confusing design
  • Provide best practice recommendations

Visual Design Elements

Final Handoff

Phase 1

We brought to life four new post types and optimized the app’s Feed screens.

Phase 2

Explore and Resort Profile screens got fresh new faces.

Phase 3

We finished up our largest app optimization project yet by creating a better User Profile and Notifications screen.

Takeaway

Working with Snowledge was my first real life native mobile app project and I learned so much about the different considerations needed there vs. a website redesign. They were a such an exciting client to explore and I loved being able to add really fun elements to their brand like the bright colors and icons.
