What's Good? Poster

White Salmon Website Redesign
Illustrated Portraits

I created this poster as part of the WWU design department's What's Good? poster contest during quarantine. I wanted to combine vintage futureistic technology with bright comic book colors, & was really inspired by illustrator Derek Yaniger's work. My friend Katrina and I have been watching movies together over video call, so I wanted to show how much it brightens my day while in social isolation!
I was recognized as one of three finalists in the contest.
