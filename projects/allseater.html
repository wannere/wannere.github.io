AllSeater engaged us in a website redesign from the ground up with full creative liscense. Beginning with wireframe creation and utilizing a ticket motif (as the team wanted to be cognisant of season tickets for non-sport events, like movie screenings and concert series), I created a multipage marketing website, an expanded brand style guide, and app notification emails.

Role

I was the lead designer for the AllSeater for both the marketing website, notification emails, and expanded brand style guide based off of existing logo and brand color.

Duration

6 weeks

Tools

Figma

Existing Site Pain Points

  • Over half the homepage was overly negative about current ticket distribution experience, only getting to AllSeater as the solution far below the fold
  • Floating orange and blue circles did not connect with the product and were primarily there to fill space
  • Hard-hitting stats (AllSeater reduces ticket waste by 30%) are located at the bottom of the page, and purpose of product was not clear until that point
  • Overall lack of thoughtful design

Goals

  • Create UX wireframes to gather evolving insights and feedback from main stakeholders (AllSeater web app was still in production and subject to change)
  • Provide full site designs for two distinct phases (MVP and the app's public launch)
  • Design brand style guide that can apply to the marketing site, notification emails, and eventually the product itself

Visual Design Elements

Final Handoff

Homepage

Because different audiences will utilize the AllSeater app in drastically different ways, it was important to easily and distinctly differentiate, allowing users to self-segment to get the information that is most relevant to them.

Pricing

Room for custom FAQs for each user type.

Sign Up

The distinct user types needed different signup forms, so the first question on the form (what type of user are you) was visually removed from the form itself and now acts as the self-segmentation tool to show the correct form.

Notification Emails

Each step of the ticket lottery system needs an email so users stay up to date on when to select tickets, how to use their tickets, and upcoming lotteries. (Note: Our engagement with AllSeater was completed before copy was finalized)

Impact

The new website is projected to drive early user engagement and retention. This project also includes the establishment of a robust brand identity, leading to increased recognition and user loyalty from the outset, especially through the interest list signup before app launch. Looking forward, the AllSeater web style guide's flexibility ensures scalability, setting the stage for seamless future updates and expansions, which will be pivotal in driving the app's success and growth.
