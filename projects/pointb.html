How can a consultant brochure site communicate connections between solutions, insights, and experts?

Summary

Point B was seeking a full website redesign based on the brand strategy XO Agency had created, which was mostly print and deck materials. I translated the branding to web, designed over 20 separate pages, and provided a 16-page design library.

Role

I worked with XO Agency as an outside resource, providing web design expertise to align with their completed brand redesign and ongoing copywriting.

Existing Site Pain Points

Too many photos of people

Over-reliance on cards to show information

Branding, color, and type are boring and outdated, not showcasing Point B's skill

Goals

Create atomic web design style guide that coordinates with existing brand redesign and print materials

Build custom components that communicate industry expertise

Utilize abstract illustrations and patterns without overdoing it
