I worked directly with XO Agency to create a full website redesign based on the brand strategy XO had provided for Point B, a consulting firm. I designed over 20 separate pages and provided a 16-page web styles guide.

Role

I worked with XO Agency as an outside resource, providing web design expertise to follow their completed brand redesign and ongoing copywriting.

Duration

4 sprints over 12 weeks

Tools

Adobe XD

Existing Site Pain Points

  • Too many photos of people
  • Over-reliance on cards to show information
  • Branding, color, and type are boring and outdated, not showcasing Point B's skill

Goals

  • Create atomic web design style guide that coordinates with existing brand redesign and print materials
  • Build custom components that communicate industry expertise
  • Utilize abstract illustrations and patterns without overdoing it

Visual Design Elements

Final Handoff

Homepage

Custom video slider, intersections graphic, and article lockup delivers a larger-than-life homepage experience.

Technology

Utilizing a different abstract illustration to animate between different technology slides packs a punch on emphasis.

Case Study

Right rail layout so readers can focus on content and impact.

Solution

A more understated intersection graphic allows users to understand how Point B connects industries and technology.

Forecasted Impact

In redesigning Point B's website, we achieved a significant transformation. The fresh design and updated branding majorly enhanced their professional image and credibility, which will likely give a boost in user engagement and set them apart from competitors. The new website is a scalable and efficient platform, with the potential to drive increased client engagement and growth.
