Rotten Tomatoes UX Redesign

White Salmon, WA Website Redesign
R.E.M. Poster

This partner UX assignment was to redesign an existing website for the near future (2025–2035) while taking into consideration user pain points, competitors, and incoming technology. My partner and I chose to redesign Rotten Tomatoes. In our research, we discovered that over 90% of surveyed users were not likely to leave reviews of their own on Rotten Tomatoes. Our goals were to integrate the review system into streaming platforms like Netflix, Hulu, and HBO, while refining the process for making and reading reviews on the site.
