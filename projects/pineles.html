WORK ABOUT RESUME

Cipe Pineles Designer Promo

Karma Surface Design

This project was to create a type-dominant promo showcasing the work or lifetime of a specific designer and invite the viewer to a hypothetical showcase of their work. I chose Cipe Pineles who was an underrated publication designer that revolutionized how mass media was marketed to teenage girls and also paved a way for women in graphic design. Watch it here.
