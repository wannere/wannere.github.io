Summary

We wanted a place on the Roboboogie website to show off examples of our work with a focus on visual design rather than in-depth strategy covered in our existing case studies. The project also needed intuitive navigation between clients and menus, new components that called back to our existing website design, and easy to understand ways to explore a variety of deliverables.

Role

I explored round 2 of UX for this project before acting as the lead designer for the UI phase.

Completed in 8 weeks with Adobe XD

User Pain Points

Roboboogie’s visual work isn’t showcased well when reading through text-heavy case studies

Goals

Create new components that called back to our existing website design

Explore easy-to-understand ways to showcase a variety of deliverables

Find intuitive navigation between main site, different clients, and new menus

Visual Design Elements

Final Handoff

Results

Our existing results page got a flashy slider to show off visual designs.

Portfolio Landing

Users are able to sort by what type of design work we completed.

Client Work

I created a library of possible components to use to fit the deliverables for each client.

Impact

Showcasing Roboboogie's past work on our website builds credibility and trust, demonstrating our expertise and successful track record. The portfolio can also stand to inspire potential clients, showcasing Robo's diverse skills and unique approach. Transparency instills confidence in prospective clients, ultimately attracting and converting more business.
