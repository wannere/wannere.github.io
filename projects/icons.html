University Residences Icons

While working on the website for WWU Housing, the design team decided that we wanted to show different types of dorms and amenities in icon format. I created these icons that follow Western's color guidelines and match the website's other icons for consistency.
The icons show different bike storage options (indoor, covered, outdoor), different bed options (stationary, loftable, bunkable), different dorm options (hall style, suite style, apartment style), elevator access, wheelchair access, and substance free communities.

Loader GIFs

In this project the assignment was to create three unique GIFs that could be used as loaders, something better than just a rotating circle or three dots that change color. We were also tasked with animating the GIFs in Photoshop frame by frame, so the results are a little choppier than if they were animated in After Effects. For my loaders, I used a bow and arrow morph, orange juice being poured, and paper being crumpled up.

Thematic Icons

This project was to create a set of six thematic icons using a limited color palette. I did some homework at a conveyor sushi restaurant and ended up with this icon set.
